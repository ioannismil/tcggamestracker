About TCG Tracker

TCG Tracker is a platform designed for all trading card game (TCG) players who want to keep a detailed record of their play sessions. Whether you're a dedicated Magic: The Gathering player, a Pokémon battler, or a Yu‑Gi‑Oh! duelist, One Piece and many more, tracking your games shouldn’t be guesswork. This app lets you visualize your progress with clear stats, graphs, and custom trackers made precisely for your format.

What You Can Do

  • Record every game result, deck, and opponent with one click.
  • Use custom-made trackers to log life points, energy, poison counters, and more.
  • Analyze your performance with interactive graphs and running win‑rates.
  • Keep a complete match history accessible on any device.

Why Sign In?

TCG Tracker uses secure Google authentication. Your data is linked only to your account no passwords stored, no unwanted sharing. Once you log in, your entire game history stays synced across devices automatically.

Our Mission

Our goal is to bring powerful yet simple stat tracking to the global TCG community. Whether you attend local tournaments or casual kitchen‑table sessions, we want every duel, match, and playtest to count.

We believe in helping players track, grow, and celebrate their improvement one match at a time.
